  • Why us?

    We do our best to bring you a cheap and reliable bot for your discord account


  • Security concerns?

    The only data we collect are hwid [Hardware Identification] to avoid sharing of licenses and the license key for authentication.
    Hwid is an alphanumerical code [ex. 03000102030405060708090a1d0c0d0e0e] that identifies the hardware
    components that the system is utilizing.
    Hover here to know more

    License keys are the codes you bought from us or a reseller and are accessible only to the developers and don't contain any
    personal information.


  • What about discord changes?

    If you are wondering what would happen when discord reduce message content access to 100 servers, we can tell you that
    basically the bot can stop working if you enter the max number of server that a user can join (100 servers).
    We are trying to find a way around that.
